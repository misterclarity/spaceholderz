Guardians of the New Sky

A Cooperative Journey of Stewardship

In this game, players co-create a new world not by becoming "more" in a heroic sense, but by letting go of attachments and certainties. Humanity is revealed through vulnerability rather than dominance.

Enlightenment is experienced as a release, not an acquisition. It is the emptiness inside that makes the vessel useful. The game's mechanics require players to act as stewards of something bigger than themselves, requiring them to drop the "trail of baggage" they've been carrying.

The Hidden Quest

At the start, each player receives a secondary quest representing a call to service—unglamorous, invisible, but essential. Spaceholders are individuals whose calling is not to create big structures or have a massive public impact, but to embody presence in small, everyday tasks like gardening, handicrafts. Whoever comes into contact with them enters the field of presence of the spaceholder. Whatever happens to one affects the collective. True healing arises from shared presence. The game's goal can thus be defined as everyone being present together, at the same time. While the surface selves are different, humans can meet in a sense of oneness at the level of depth. Since the game is cooperative, it can only be won by working together on both the visible, stated goals and the invisible acts of stewardship.

Media Bias Analysis: Analyze news articles and media content to identify astrological symbolism and archetypal bias embedded in the language—revealing the cosmic fingerprint behind editorial perspective.

Author Profiling: Identify a writer's likely astrological placements from their books, essays, or body of work. Linguistic patterns, thematic obsessions, and narrative structures all carry signatures of the natal chart.

Archetype Extraction: Extract and map astrological archetypes from multimedia works such as films, music, and visual art—uncovering the mythological blueprints that give a work its resonance and cultural power.

AstroStudies

Non-Linear Lifelong Learning

AstroStudies is a personalized, non-linear education service that uses a person's astrological data to generate the optimal study curriculum across their entire lifetime. Rather than following rigid, one-size-fits-all academic paths, AstroStudies maps each individual's innate talents, learning rhythms, and developmental windows to create a living curriculum that evolves with them.

Who Is It For?

Young Explorers: High school students—or even younger—who sense that the conventional education system doesn't reflect who they truly are. AstroStudies helps them discover their natural aptitudes and passions before they're forced into premature choices.

People Seeking a New Direction: Anyone feeling stuck or uninspired in their professional or personal path. By consulting the natal chart's evolving transits and progressions, AstroStudy identifies the ideal moments to pivot, deepen, or explore entirely new fields of knowledge.

How It Works: Your birth chart becomes the foundation of a dynamic learning map. Planetary cycles reveal when to study logic vs. creativity, when to specialize vs. broaden, and which subjects will unlock the deepest growth at each life stage. The result is an education that honors both timing and individuality—a curriculum written in the stars.
