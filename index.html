Who We Are

Spaceholderz.com is a distributed mental health community. We empower members to create and provide meaningful support to one another through coaching skills. Our community forms a network of trained peer supporters who can offer contextually relevant help when needed. Our members are primarily young adults between 11 and 23 years old.

Our Philosophy

At Spaceholderz, we believe that focusing on understanding, creating, and maintaining optimal attitudes is needed for a rewarding life. We also emphasize that skills are more important than knowledge, especially in an increasingly information-rich age.

We strive for a world where optimal mental health is created by a network of conscious and skilled individuals maintaining homeostasis, rather than relying solely on a handful of professionals. We envision a societal fascia that supports everyone through a culture of co-regulatory and self-regulatory equilibrium.

We call this swarm healing.

How Spaceholding Works

Spaceholding leverages:

Existing community relationships
Evidence-based practices (such as NVC, mindfulness)
Human connection

Skills We Learn

Spaceholderz learn fundamental coaching skills, including:

Reflective listening
Asking clarifying questions
Maintaining mindful presence
Facilitating safe spaces

Outcomes of Spaceholding

Through our approach, we foster key outcomes:

Congruence - realness, genuineness, transparency, authenticity, openness
Acceptance - respect or unconditional positive regard
Empathic understanding - deep understanding for feelings and meanings

Benefits of Our Approach

This approach helps to:

Enhance community resilience
Reduce social isolation
Improve mental well-being
Create authentic connections

Establishing Spaceholding

Key steps to establish Spaceholding include:

Train members

Ongoing learning
